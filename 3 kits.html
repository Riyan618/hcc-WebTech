REAL MADRID 23-24 HOME KIT

The Real Madrid 23-24 home kit has a modern yet simple design. What is most interesting is the colorway - white is combined with a dark navy ('Legend Ink') and a goldish orange.The goldish orange color is neither pure yellow nor metallic gold but somewhere between it. The navy and orange-golden are the same colors that are also used for the Real Madrid 23-24 away kit.For the first time in the club's history, the Real Madrid 23-24 home kit features the club's slogan "Â¡Hala Madrid!" on the collar. For the collar, Adidas combine the "goldish-orange", White & Legend Ink into a Tricolor. The sleeve cuffs also come with the Tricolor.Just as all other Adidas 23-24 soccer jerseys, the new Real Madrid 23-24 home kit boasts the new Adidas logo. On the side and the back, there are navy panels, similar to some Adidas 2022 World Cup kits.

PSG 23-24 Home Kit

The Nike Paris SG 2023-2024 home football shirt evokes memories of PSG's jerseys of the early 2000s, worn by the likes of Ronaldinho and Gabriel Heinze.The Nike PSG 2023-24 home shirt is navy with a red stripe on the left that fades toward the bottom of the shirt. There is also a fading white stripe on the left and right of the red stripe, inspired by the team's classic Hechter design.The fading design is inspired by how the Parisian skyline fades into the night sky. Interestingly, the Swoosh is red and not white. All other sponsor logos are white.Blue shorts and socks complete the Nike PSG 23-24 home kit.
